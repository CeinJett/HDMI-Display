## Display Connector Pinout

Pin    | Signal
------ | ------
     1 | LED- (negative)
     2 | LED+ (positive)
     3 | Gnd
     4 | 3V3
  5-12 | R0-R7
 13-20 | G0-G7
 21-28 | B0-B7
    29 | Gnd
    30 | Clk
    31 | Disp (low=off, high=on)
    32 | HSync
    33 | VSync
    34 | DE (Data Enable)
    35 | NC (Not Connected)
    36 | Gnd
    37 | XR/X+
    38 | YD/Y-
    39 | XL/X-
    40 | YU/Y+
